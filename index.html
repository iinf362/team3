Our Purpose

The laws related to the landlord-tenant relationship in New York State are complex. This site will help you navigate those laws.

Landlord tenant relationships before and after

It covers the laws related to creating a landlord-tenant relationship, like when you are looking to rent an apartment and the agreement you will reach once you do so. Finally, it will cover the laws related to ending a landlord-tenant relationship and the eviction process.

Repairs

It also covers the rules that protect tenants once they are in an apartment, like the duties the landlord owes the tenant to keep the apartment or home that is rented in good repair.

Useful Resources

You will also find useful links that can help you find a lawyer and give you more information about the laws addressed on this site as well as the government agencies that can help tenants who are having problems with their landlords.
